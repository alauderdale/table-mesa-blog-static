This Is Our Story

Many healthcare organizations face the challenge of having to manually verify license status with the Primary Source. EverCheck is the result of recognizing an opportunity within that challenge, and offering a solution that makes sense for hospitals’ compliance requirements and for their budgets. CE Broker, Florida’s official healthcare continuing education tracking system and EverCheck’s parent company, began providing license verification services in 2005. Since then, we’ve grown to 20+ employees who service more than 200 hospitals nationwide. And we’re just getting started.
