Collaborate With Your Team. Like a Boss.

Bring your team together with EverCheck’s collaborative tools. Delegate tasks, resolve action items quickly, and share important files. Everyone is on the same page, and it’s all documented inside EverCheck.

Task Assignment

Keep track of who is responsible for resolving license issues and eliminate risky assumptions. With our task assignment feature, transparency and accountability are at the forefront. Supervisors can delegate action items to other users, set deadlines, and manage results.

Notes

When you have something to say, put it on the record. With our notes feature, you can leave comments under specific to-dos or employee files and have a dated record of who said what.

File Sharing

Don’t waste time searching for important documents. With our file sharing feature, you can attach important documents directly to the employee file. You have access, your team has access, and everyone knows exactly where to look.
