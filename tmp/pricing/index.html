Questions?

We’re here to help you find the solution that’s right for your organization. Take a look at the questions we hear most often, and speak to an EverCheck Account Executive to see which solution makes the most sense for your needs.

Do we pay for the number of licensed employees or the total number of licenses?

The number of licensed employees. Pay one flat rate for each employee, regardless of the number of licenses.

How are employees added or removed from billing?

Employees are added or removed via a simple automated refresh process, and your organization is billed only for employees whose licenses have been verified within a given month. Employees’ existing data will remain in EverCheck forever.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. It’s our responsibility to keep our customers satisfied. Stay because you love our service, not because you have to.

Are there additional fees for implementation?

No. Implementation is and always will be included.
